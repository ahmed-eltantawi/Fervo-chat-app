Delete Your Data

Fervo does not save your personal data on our servers. However, per Facebook policy, we provide these instructions to remove your connected app activity.

  1. Go to your Facebook Account's "Settings & Privacy", then click "Settings".
  2. Find "Apps and Websites" — this shows all apps and websites linked to your Facebook account.
  3. Search for "Fervo" in the search bar and click on it.
  4. Scroll down and click "Remove".
  5. ✓  You have successfully removed your Fervo app activity from Facebook.
